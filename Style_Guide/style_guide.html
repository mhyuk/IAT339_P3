Project

PROJECT#1
Hospiration
Who is Kudoz
Kudoz is a non-profit organization and their mission is to help people with cognitive disavilities tackle the poverty of expectations and opportunities that limit's their aspirations

Design Focus
This project focuses on ethnography research methods and aims to offer alternative options for Kudoz to assist Hosts in discovering and planning for new ideas to host, reducing the likelihood of Hosts becoming inactive due to the lack of new ideas to host

Process
Ethonography Research
Ethonography research in this project requires our team to actively observe and participate in Kudoz's events. Building journey maps, personas, and workshops to understand Kudoz's situation on which is lacking.

PROJECT#2
Cage Homes
Project Brief
The website introduce how the cage homes started, specifically shown by contents and research data. In order to why Cage homes is not a solution to Hong Kong's overpopulation issue, we created infographics, animated map, and detailed information to the causes and solutions to improve Hong Kong's living standard.

Process
Research
The first stages of creating this project was to research on the topic of Cage Homes. We started our research by looking at why Cage homes is not a solution to Hong Kong's overpopulation issue and what caused this problem. The next step was establishing our target audience of our final website and the possible stakeholders and personas that would be interested in learning the issue of Cage Homes.

Flow
The second stage is to carefully plan out the flow of information delivered to the user when they are browsing through the website. This can be done by creating a site map and an information architecture diagram.
