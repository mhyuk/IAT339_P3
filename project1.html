Who is Kudoz
Kudoz is a non-profit organization and their mission is to help people with cognitive disavilities tackle the poverty of expectations and opportunities that limit's their aspirations

Design Focus
This project focuses on ethnography research methods and aims to offer alternative options for Kudoz to assist Hosts in discovering and planning for new ideas to host, reducing the likelihood of Hosts becoming inactive due to the lack of new ideas to host

Process
Ethonography Research
Ethonography research in this project requires our team to actively observe and participate in Kudoz's events. Building journey maps, personas, and workshops to understand Kudoz's situation on which is lacking.
