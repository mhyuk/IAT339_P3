PROJECT#1
Hospiration
A mobile application to help host find more inspirations for planning Experiences


Who is Kudoz
Kudoz is a non-profit organization that tries to help people with cognitive disabilities to explore potentials of themselves, build up motivations and shape a path for their future through an experience platform

Design Focus
Offering alternative options to assist Hosts in discovering and planning for new ideas to host, reducing the likelihood of Hosts becoming inactive due to the lack of new ideas to host.

Team
Our group consist of 6 SIAT major students

My Role
- Ethnography research (Interviews, Preparing Workshops, Observing Hosting Sessions)
- Graphics Design (Poster/Slides layout, Icons, UX/UI)
- Brainstorming ideas and Wireframes


Process
Ethnography Research
Ethnography research in this project requires our team to actively observe and participate in Kudoz's events. Building journey maps, personas, and workshops to understand Kudoz's situation on which is lacking.

Findings
- Insufficient of active Host
- Lack of New Ideas

Problem
- Inactive Hosts
- High Host Dropping Rate
- Lack of New Ideas

Conclusion
Throughout the process of working on this project, our group was able to learn the methods of using ethnography research to bring short and long term value to Kudoz. Values for Host such as reduce stress on discovering and planning their Experiences workshops and build a stronger resume for their future opportunities. Values for Kudoz's staff such as having a less steep Host drop rate, stabilizing the host community, and reducing the burden in recruiting and preparing Hosts.
